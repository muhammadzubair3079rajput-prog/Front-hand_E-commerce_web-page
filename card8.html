9mm Bullets

Compact, reliable, and widely used ammunition ideal for pistols and submachine guns.

9mm Bullet Specifications

The 9×19mm round is one of the most popular and versatile ammunition types in the world, known for its balance of recoil control, stopping power, and accuracy.

  • Caliber: 9×19mm
  • Bullet Weight: 115–147 grains
  • Muzzle Velocity: 350–400 m/s
  • Use Case: Pistols, SMGs, compact defense platforms
  • Design: Full metal jacket or hollow point variants
